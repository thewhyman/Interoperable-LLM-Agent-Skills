## 2. Leaders by Thesis

### 🚀 Asymmetric Upside: **Aztec Network**
*   **Thesis**: The "Privacy L2" everyone waited years for is finally liquid.
*   **Why**: Token tradable date **Feb 11, 2026** (next week). This is a pure repricing event.
*   **Risk**: Post-unlock dumps are common; volatility will be extreme.

### 🏟️ Real Usage Today: **Ronin & Immutable**
*   **Thesis**: If you want exposure to gamers actually playing, you go here.
*   **Stats**: Ronin (~536k MAU) and Immutable (~640k MAU) dwarf most general purpose L2s in sticky user behavior.
*   **Play**: Defensive growth.

### 🧪 Highest Tech Risk/Reward: **Fhenix**
*   **Thesis**: "Fully Homomorphic Encryption" (FHE) is the 2026 buzzword replacing 2024's "ZK".
*   **Why**: Enormous promise (compute on encrypted data) but unproven at scale. If it works, it captures the "Dark Finance" market.

### 📉 Needs Proof Soon: **Manta Pacific**
*   **Thesis**: First mover advantage has eroded.
*   **Why**: TVL collapsed from $1.7B peaks to ~$123M today. Needs a massive roadmap pivot or killer privacy dApp to reverse the trend.

## 4. Notes & Caveats

*   **Privacy Valuations**: Historically, privacy coins underperform in bull runs until "regulatory fear" kicks in. The 2026 narrative might shift this if "Institutional Privacy" (Midnight/Aztec) takes off.
*   **Gaming Metrics**: Be skeptical of SKALE/Xai "active user" numbers; verify with "active value" (fees paid). High bot activity is common in low-fee chains.
*   **General L2s**: Base remains the baseline "Consumer L2". If a gaming chain isn't growing faster than Base, it's losing.
